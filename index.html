Mindfulness Breathing Therapy

What is mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the basic human ability to be fully present, aware of where we are and what we’re doing, and not overly reactive or overwhelmed by what’s going on around us.

What are the benefits of mindfulness?

Researchers theorize that mindfulness meditation promotes metacognitive awareness, decreases anxiety via disengagement from perseverative cognitive activities and enhances attentional capacities through gains in working memory. These cognitive gains, in turn, contribute to effective emotion-regulation strategies.

How do I practice Mindfulness with Dreamlight?

  1. Take a moment to sit in a comfortable position or lie down.
  2. Take a few deep breaths to relax your body and your mind. Slowly adjust your breaths in accordance with Dreamlight pre-programmed inner pulsing lights.
  3. When light increases, slowly inhale; when light decreases, slowly exhale. Do it repeatedly for at least 5 minutes.
  • *Mindfulness is recommended by the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) for many conditions including various mental illnesses, chronic pain, insomnia and stress.
  • *Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction is used for Primary Chronic Insomnia, according to a NCBI study( US National Library of Medicine and National Institutes of Health).
  • *Studies show that deep breathing elicits a feeling of relaxation, according to an article carried by Harvard Medical school.
