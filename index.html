What's dreamlight

Dreamlight is a creative smart system to help you get faster enhanced sleep and it can create an ideal atmosphere to help you meditate.

We use three proven effective ways to make users fall asleep faster.

  • Orange light to guide your breathing, help you to focus and fall asleep.
  • Stereo Bluetooth speakers, gives you the sound of nature, white noise, light music or any other music you like which makes you feel immersed in a real nature environment.
  • Vocal hypnosis therapy, a soft voice leads you to relax and fall asleep naturally.

These three features could work separately or together, so you have multiple choices to find your favorite way

Dreamlight Pro is a helpful device to assist your meditation. It’s a perfect fit for your face, keep your eyes open and you will feel that you are in an isolated place. Mindfulness breathing lights lead you to focus on your breathing, meditation music helps to generate a Zen chamber and the training program we are working on will help you practice meditation efficiently.
